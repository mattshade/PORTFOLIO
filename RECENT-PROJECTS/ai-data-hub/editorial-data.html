👁️ Dataset Overview

This dataset contains a comprehensive archive of consumable content including articles, cards, videos, images, recipes, and other editorial assets. It serves as the canonical source of truth for content metadata, versioning, and lineage across the organization.

Scope & coverage: Data spans from June 2005 to present with 21.3 million total versions across 5.5 million documents. Content is US-centric and includes support entities such as authors, taxonomy, organizations, persons, and products. One record per document version with full change history.

Key capabilities: Sophisticated versioning model supporting as-of views for historical analysis. Enables content gap analysis, trend detection, author attribution, and taxonomy-driven content discovery. Join keys available for integration with performance and engagement datasets.

Best suited for: Content strategy, editorial planning, author performance analysis, taxonomy management, and historical content trend analysis.
