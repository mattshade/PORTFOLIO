👁️ Dataset Overview

Comprehensive e-commerce and retail analytics including transactional data, cart abandonment metrics, conversion funnels, and revenue by product category. This dataset powers purchase analytics, merchandising optimization, and customer lifecycle modeling across the full path from browse to buy.

Scope & coverage: Supports segmentation by customer lifecycle, geography, device, and product category. Includes AOV, LTV, cohort retention, cart abandonment rates, and multi-touch attribution. Session-level and order-level granularity with full product hierarchy.

Data quality & freshness: Refreshed hourly with complete order and session data. Full attribution tracking and join keys to audience and campaign datasets for unified analytics.

Best suited for: Revenue forecasting, merchandising and assortment planning, conversion optimization, LTV modeling, and cross-channel attribution.
