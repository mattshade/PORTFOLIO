👁️ Dataset Overview

Campaign-level advertising metrics from Google Ads, Facebook Ads, LinkedIn, and Twitter enable ROAS optimization, audience comparison, and creative performance analysis. This dataset is the source of truth for paid media spend, conversions, and efficiency benchmarking.

Scope & coverage: Impressions, clicks, CTR, cost, and conversions at the campaign level. Supports breakdown by platform, audience segment, creative format, and date. Includes attribution windows and optional creative metadata (format, copy length) where available.

Data quality & freshness: ~678K campaigns refreshed daily. Cost in USD; conversions use platform-defined attribution. Cross-platform CPA and ROAS comparable when segment definitions align; join keys to audience and conversion datasets for unified analytics.

Best suited for: Budget allocation, audience targeting optimization, creative A/B analysis, ROAS and CPA benchmarking, and cross-channel attribution modeling.
