👁️ Dataset Overview

Episode-level podcast analytics across major platforms power content strategy, length optimization, and geographic expansion. This dataset answers which episodes perform, how long listeners stay, and where audiences are concentrated.

Scope & coverage: Downloads, average listen duration, completion rate, and country per episode. Includes podcast name, genre, episode length, and release date. Supports aggregation by genre, length bucket, release day, and geography. 48 countries represented with US, UK, Canada, Australia leading.

Data quality & freshness: ~892K episodes refreshed weekly. Completion rates and listen duration normalized for episode length; geographic coverage varies by platform. Subscriber and growth metrics available where platforms expose them.

Best suited for: Episode length and format optimization, genre performance comparison, geographic expansion planning, ad placement timing, and podcast portfolio strategy.
