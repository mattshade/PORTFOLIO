👁️ Dataset Overview

Rich demographic and behavioral data providing deep insights into audience segments. This dataset enables precise audience targeting, personalization strategies, and reach planning across all channels. It is the foundational layer for campaign optimization, content recommendations, and customer lifetime value modeling.

Scope & coverage: Includes age, gender, location (ZIP+4 for 80% of US users), interests, consumption patterns, device preferences, and engagement tiers. Supports 850 interest segments and 50-state geographic coverage. Cross-tabulation is supported across all dimensions with no cardinality limits.

Data quality & freshness: Refreshed daily with a 30-day rolling lookback. 99% of profiles have complete demographic coverage. All records include consent flags and suppression rules for compliant activation.

Best suited for: Personalization engines, segment-based A/B testing, reach and frequency modeling, and LTV/customer analytics.
