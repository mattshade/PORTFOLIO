👁️ Dataset Overview

Post-level engagement metrics from Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn power content optimization, scheduling models, and audience insights. This dataset is the primary source for understanding what performs across platforms, when to post, and which formats drive the most reach and interaction.

Scope & coverage: 12-month rolling window with likes, shares, comments, reach, and engagement rate at the post level. Includes content type (video, image, carousel, text), posting timestamp, and platform. Supports aggregation by platform, day-of-week, hour, content format, and audience segment where available.

Data quality & freshness: Refreshed daily with ~2.45M records. Platform-specific API limits apply; missing data flagged. Temporal coverage enables seasonal and trend analysis; join keys available for content and campaign datasets.

Best suited for: Content calendar optimization, format benchmarking, platform mix strategy, engagement trend analysis, and social ROI attribution.
