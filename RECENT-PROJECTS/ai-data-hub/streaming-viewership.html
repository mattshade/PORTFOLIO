👁️ Dataset Overview

Session-level viewership data from Netflix, Hulu, Amazon Prime, and Disney+ enables content performance analysis, retention modeling, and device-based optimization. Essential for understanding how audiences consume streaming content, where they drop off, and what drives completion.

Scope & coverage: Watch time (minutes), completion rate, device (Smart TV, mobile, laptop, tablet), age group, and platform per session. Content metadata includes title, genre, and original vs licensed. Supports analysis by platform, device, demographic, content type, and time of day.

Data quality & freshness: ~5.5M sessions with daily refresh. Completion rates normalized across variable content length; device and age dimensions available for 94% of records. Join keys for content catalog enable title-level performance rollups.

Best suited for: Content acquisition and licensing decisions, retention and churn modeling, device strategy, audience segmentation for ads, and programming schedule optimization.
