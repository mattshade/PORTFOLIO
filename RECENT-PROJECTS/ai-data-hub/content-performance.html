👁️ Dataset Overview

Comprehensive content performance data spanning blogs, videos, infographics, and interactive content. This dataset unifies engagement and conversion signals across all distribution channels to power content optimization, format benchmarking, and ROI attribution.

Scope & coverage: Tracks time-on-page, scroll depth, video completion rates, and conversion attribution by content piece, format, channel, and audience segment. Supports cross-format comparison (e.g., video vs. article performance) and funnel analysis from impression to conversion.

Data quality & freshness: Refreshed daily with 90-day retention. Full session-level detail with cookie and user-ID linkage for deduplication and attribution modeling.

Best suited for: Content strategy, format mix optimization, channel performance comparison, engagement benchmarking, and conversion funnel analysis.
